Turning Ideas Into

Founded in 2016, Fresh Ideas Consulting is a 100% black female-youth-owned BBBEE consultancy dedicated to empowering businesses across South Africa. With a strong focus on innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth, we help businesses overcome challenges, optimize operations, and unlock their full potential.

Through our strategic approach, we have transformed underperforming SMMEs into thriving enterprises by equipping them with the tools, knowledge, and mentorship they need to compete in today’s evolving markets. Our impact extends beyond businesses—we drive economic inclusion, job creation, and industry transformation.
