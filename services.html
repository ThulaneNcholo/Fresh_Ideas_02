Enterprise Development Program

Our Enterprise Development Program is designed to empower emerging businesses by equipping them with the tools, skills, and resources needed to thrive. By supporting entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey, we aim to create a pipeline of successful, sustainable businesses. Key components of this program include:

Facilitating access to funding and resources for growth.

Assisting with market readiness to secure supply contracts.

Providing mentorship and capacity-building initiatives that enhance business performance.

Supplier Development Program

We work with businesses to build a reliable and competitive supplier network, enabling them to comply with BBBEE requirements while creating lasting value. Our Supplier Development Program focuses on:

Facilitating access to funding and resources for growth.

Driving innovation through supplier performance improvement.

Strengthening relationships between corporates and their supplier base to ensure long-term collaboration.

Socio-Economic Development (SED)

Our SED initiatives are aimed at uplifting disadvantaged communities by promoting sustainable development and social impact. These initiatives include:

Supporting education, skills training, and job creation efforts.

Collaborating with businesses to develop impactful SED projects aligned with their strategic goals.

Monitoring and measuring the outcomes of SED initiatives to ensure effectiveness.

Business Incubation

Our business incubation services accelerate entrepreneurial success by:

Providing access to supply contracts and linking entrepreneurs with procurement opportunities.

Assisting with funding and resource allocation to drive business growth.

Enhancing professional identities through branding, logo creation, website development, and company profile design.

SMME Development

We are passionate about helping small, medium, and micro enterprises (SMMEs) grow and succeed. Our approach includes:

Conducting comprehensive skills gap analyses and offering targeted mentoring programs.

Implementing sustainability initiatives such as business-to-business coaching, tender application support, and digital presence optimization to enhance competitiveness.

Procurement Support

We help businesses align their procurement practices with BBBEE requirements while ensuring long-term sustainability by:

Creating competitive, supply-chain-ready enterprises with future-proof capabilities.

Shifting the focus from mere compliance to fostering impactful and sustainable supplier relationships.

Strategic Consulting

We provide strategic consulting to maximize BBBEE compliance benefits while minimizing costs through:

Scenario planning and transformation strategies tailored to your business needs.

Optimizing BBBEE compliance strategies to achieve both immediate and long-term goals.

BBBEE Strategy Development

We design comprehensive BBBEE strategies that align with your business objectives, focusing on:

Building long-term value for businesses and their stakeholders.

Identifying opportunities to maximize compliance benefits while creating meaningful transformation impacts.

Workplace Skills Plan Preparation

We assist businesses in developing and implementing workplace skills plans (WSPs) that:

Align with their strategic goals and transformation priorities.

Unlock funding opportunities through Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs).

BBBEE Audit Assistance

We provide audit readiness services to ensure seamless BBBEE verification processes, including:

Preparing documentation and evidence required for audits.

Conducting pre-audit assessments to identify and address gaps.

Offering support throughout the verification process to achieve optimal outcomes.
