Enterprise Development Program

Enterprise Development Program

Our Enterprise Development Program is designed to empower emerging businesses by equipping them with the tools, skills, and resources needed to thrive. By supporting entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey, we aim to create a pipeline of successful, sustainable businesses. Key components of this program include:

Facilitating access to funding and resources for growth.

Assisting with market readiness to secure supply contracts.

Providing mentorship and capacity-building initiatives that enhance business performance.

Supplier Development Program

Supplier Development Program

We work with businesses to build a reliable and competitive supplier network, enabling them to comply with BBBEE requirements while creating lasting value. Our Supplier Development Program focuses on:

Facilitating access to funding and resources for growth.

Driving innovation through supplier performance improvement.

Strengthening relationships between corporates and their supplier base to ensure long-term collaboration.

Socio-Economic Development (SED)

Socio-Economic Development (SED)

Our SED initiatives are aimed at uplifting disadvantaged communities by promoting sustainable development and social impact. These initiatives include:

Supporting education, skills training, and job creation efforts.

Collaborating with businesses to develop impactful SED projects aligned with their strategic goals.

Monitoring and measuring the outcomes of SED initiatives to ensure effectiveness.

Business Incubation

Business Incubation

Our business incubation services accelerate entrepreneurial success by:
